# NeuralMarket Protocol Amendment 001 — Market Data and Chronological Splits

Date: 2026-07-12
Base commit: 349a5b3 (reproducible research foundation)
Status: source datasets and chronological splits specified and qualified against
the study account; historical data not yet acquired. This amendment does not alter
the hypotheses, endpoints, or success metrics of Research Protocol v1.

## Reason for amendment

Protocol v1 deferred exact chronological split dates until data availability and
legal access were confirmed. This amendment qualifies a concrete market-data
source, defines canonical data contracts, and freezes the chronological split
boundaries so that model training may later begin against fixed rules.

## Provider and datasets

Provider: Databento.

- Underlying: `ARCX.PILLAR`, symbol `SPY` (`raw_symbol`).
  - `definition`, `ohlcv-1d` (daily bars), `bbo-1m` (venue quotes); optional
    `statistics`.
- Option definitions: `OPRA.PILLAR` / `definition`, parent selector `SPY.OPT`
  (`parent`).
- Option quotes: `OPRA.PILLAR` / `cbbo-1m`, parent selector `SPY.OPT`.

### Underlying venue selection (ARCX.PILLAR)

On this account, only single-venue Pillar feeds provide SPY history back to the
Databento US-equities Pillar inception (2018-05-01); every consolidated equities
feed (`EQUS.SUMMARY`, `EQUS.MINI`, `DBEQ.BASIC`) begins in 2023 or 2024. SPY is
primary-listed on NYSE Arca, so `ARCX.PILLAR` is used as the primary-listing-venue
reference path. This is an approved, documented venue-specific choice with these
limitations recorded in the source manifest:

- `ARCX.PILLAR` is venue-specific NYSE Arca data, not SIP or consolidated equities.
- ARCX BBO is not official NBBO and ARCX volume is not full-market SPY volume; the
  ARCX spread role is auxiliary and transaction costs use explicitly modeled costs.
- Acceptance is conditional on later row-level quality, corporate-action, and
  venue-proxy validation.
- The primary-listing-venue exception applies only to SPY; other underlyings
  require separate qualification.

## Canonical schemas

Provider-neutral contracts: `UnderlyingDailyBar`, `UnderlyingQuoteSnapshot`,
`OptionDefinition`, `OptionQuoteSnapshot`, and `RejectedRecord`, each versioned
(`1.0`) and expressed as a typed model, a PyArrow storage schema, and a JSON Schema
under `data_contracts/`.

## Point-in-time definitions

Option definitions are stored point-in-time (as known at each
`definition_timestamp`) to prevent contract-listing lookahead. They are not
collapsed into a single final-state table.

## End-of-day option quote snapshot rule

> Use the final valid consolidated quote at or before 15:59:00 America/New_York,
> subject to a maximum quote age of five minutes.

Crossed quotes are rejected; locked quotes are retained and flagged; a missing
side is rejected.

## Study range

2018-05-01 through 2025-12-31, inclusive, for SPY daily underlying, point-in-time
SPY option definitions, and SPY consolidated interval quotes. The provider request
interval is half-open, `[2018-05-01, 2026-01-01)`.

## Frozen chronological splits

Computed on the XNYS calendar (`exchange-calendars` 4.13.2) with a 60-session
maximum lookback, 30-session maximum maturity, 90-session purge, and 10-session
embargo (100-session boundary exclusion). Generated by
`neuralmarket data split freeze`.

| Split | Start | End | Sessions |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Training | 2018-05-01 | 2021-12-31 | 926 |
| Excluded boundary | 2022-01-03 | 2022-05-25 | 100 |
| Validation | 2022-05-26 | 2023-06-30 | 275 |
| Excluded boundary | 2023-07-03 | 2023-11-21 | 100 |
| Final test (sealed) | 2023-11-22 | 2025-12-31 | 528 |

- Purge rationale: 90 sessions removes overlap from the 60-session lookback plus
  30-session maximum maturity, preventing shared information across splits.
- Embargo: 10 additional sessions buffer residual serial correlation.
- `final_test_access_status = "sealed"`. Final-test values are not observed during
  development. The seal is broken only after models, metrics, baselines,
  transaction costs, and statistical procedures are frozen.

Qualification is executed by `neuralmarket data qualify` using a local
`DATABENTO_API_KEY`, issuing only metadata, symbology, and cost-estimation
requests under a download guard. Result: **qualified**. Stages A–E (discovery,
schema, coverage, symbology, cost) all passed.

- Underlying `ARCX.PILLAR` / `SPY`: symbology status normalized to `OK` (raw `0`,
  message `OK`), one complete mapping interval, no uncovered interval, resolved.
- Options `OPRA.PILLAR` / `SPY.OPT` (`parent`): validated by
  `chunked_symbology_resolution`. Direct `parent`→`instrument_id` resolution is

  `[2018-05-01, 2026-01-01)`. The parent selector expands into child-contract
  keys; each chunk was accepted (normalized status `0` or `1`, no `not_found`),

## License and redistribution restrictions

The dataset is licensed, commercial market data. Raw and normalized records are
never redistributed through Git. Only metadata, manifests, schemas, hashes, and
small synthetic test fixtures are committed. Users must independently comply with
vendor terms.

## Conditions that invalidate this amendment

- Any required schema does not cover the full 2018-05-01..2025-12-31 window.
- OPRA entitlement is unavailable for the study account.
- SPY or the SPY option-parent selector cannot be resolved for the study range.
- Definition coverage differs materially from quote coverage.
- Full-study cost cannot be estimated or is financially unreasonable.
- Vendor licensing does not permit the intended research use.

If any condition holds, the study dates are not silently changed; a later
amendment decides whether to change vendor, schema, or study period.
